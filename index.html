How Habits Are Formed

A simple 3-step flowchart
1. Cue
A trigger or signal that initiates the habit.
(e.g., alarm rings)
2. Routine
The action or behavior you perform.
(e.g., go for a run)
3. Reward
The benefit you gain, reinforcing the habit.
(e.g., feel energized)
Repeat this loop and your habit grows stronger!
